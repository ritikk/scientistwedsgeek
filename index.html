Our Story

I, Ritik, would probably end up telling you statistics of how likely it is that two childhood friends end up getting married. Niharika on the other hand is more likely to tell you the behavior and why two childhood friends end up getting married. Together we can paint quite the picture on the subject :) Why am I bringing this up you ask? Well therein lies our story.

Niharika and I first met when we were 7-8 year old kids running around in our shorts, splashing water and building sandcastles on the beaches of Murud. The parents being friends had picked quite the place for some sun and sand and I believe more so for a break from us kids. Already a child chess prodigy by then, Niharika was in the limelight throughout the trip for being crowned one of the youngest chess champions and for her Urmila like moves on the songs of Rangeela. Having an advantage of numbers though, the "Boys" left the girls behind and went alone to do cool things like "having a Frooti" (a mango based beverage very popular amongst kids during the early 90s). Being no stranger to being left out thanks to a wonderful elder sibling, I would conceal and bring a Frooti back for Niharika every once in a while (or at least that's what I thought).

Fate crossed our paths again when I finished my engineering and moved to Pune to start my job at Cybage. Niharika was pursuing her MA in Psychology from Fergusson College at the time and thanks to Orkut (yes, you read that correctly, Orkut) and Gtalk we'd managed to get back in touch and had decided to meet up when I reached the city. This time there was no need for sports fame, dance moves or mango beverages though. That day onwards, we kept looking for excuses and ways to bring our lives together to this point here today :)

Join us in our celebrations as we begin the rest of our lives together.
